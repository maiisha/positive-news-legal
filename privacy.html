Privacy Policy – Positive News

Positive News (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

  • No accounts: The App does not require registration and does not collect personal details.
  • Local storage only: Bookmarks are stored on your device and never sent to our servers.
  • No tracking: The App does not collect or share analytics, identifiers, or sensitive information.

2. How We Use Information

Since no personal data is collected, the App does not use your information beyond what is stored locally on your device for bookmarks.

3. External Links

The App displays headlines and links to third-party news websites. When you follow a link, you are subject to that website’s terms and policies. We are not responsible for external content or privacy practices.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your data. No personal information is transmitted to us or third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be shown in the App and, if required, in the app stores.
